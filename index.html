Welcome!


For this portfolio assignment I wanted to base this project around the Petting Farm at Dominos Farms. I worked there for multiple Summers and have many fond memories being an employee there. The goal of this portfolio is to highlight the various feature and activities present at the farm!


The petting farm is a non-profit organization where vistors can enjoy an interactive and fun day. It is home to various animals that are spread across over 25 acres of land.


Admission to the farm is $6 for general admission, $5.50 for seniors, $3 for military and first responders, and free for children under the age of 2. There are also options to buy feed for the animals or a ticket for a ride on the tractor.
